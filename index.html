F² MicroTools

This site is an index to a series of small tools that are meant to be easy, simple tools to respond to a simple need.

  1. LocalSlideshow lets you open a local folder few (or a lot) of image files and it lets you watch them, with a few options such as random and different speed.
  2. LocalPhotoSphere lets you open photosphere locally to view it.
  3. LocalVideoWall lets you open a bunch of local video files and watch them all at once.
  4. LocalAudioWall lets you open a bunch of local audio files and play them all at once.

If you want to know more about me, check out my profile site: https://francoisfournier.eu
